Pi-Hole on Raspberry Pi 3

19th February 2023 / 08:00 PM

I found a nice use case for my Raspberry Pi 3: A Pi-Hole! It acts as a local DNS-Server, and has a list of domains to block, so I get less advertising when using my webbrowser. It also blocks various trackers.
